## Key Insights
- Overall subscription rate is low (~11%), indicating inefficient mass targeting
- Students and retired customers show higher conversion rates
- Longer call durations are strongly associated with successful subscriptions
- Fewer, well-targeted campaign contacts perform better than repeated follow-ups

## Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing efforts on high-conversion segments (students, retired)
- Improve call quality instead of increasing call frequency
